Something About me...

I'm Gil - I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering and a Master in Computer Engineering degree from Colorado Technical University in Colorado Springs, Colorado.

I am an Air Force veteran with nine and a half years of active duty experience. I fell in love with technology while serving in the military. I had the opportunity to work for a government agency in Colorado Springs, Colorado while pursuing my degrees. After graduation, I accepted an offer and eventually moved to the east coast and spent about four years to pursue my dream.


I have enrolled in the Coding Bootcamp at University of Denver to enhance my coding skills and pursue a career in software development. Feel free to take a look at my most recent projects in my portfolio. If you want to be in touch please visit my contact page.

About Me

I'm Gil - I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering and a Master of Science in Computer Engineering from Colorado Technical University in Colorado Springs, Colorado.


I fell in love with technology after serving the Air Force for nine and a half years, that led me in pursuing an engineering degrees. While I was studying, I was fortunate to get some exposure and worked for a government agency as a Systems Engineer. Consequently, after my Master's degree; I was offered another job by another government agency in the east coast utilizing my Computer Engineering skills.


I have enrolled in the Coding Bootcamp at University of Denver to further my coding and skills. Feel free to browse my most recent projects on my Portfolio page. Dont forget to say hello by visiting my contact page.
